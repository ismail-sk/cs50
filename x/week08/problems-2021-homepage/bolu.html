You can take a deep breath and go on a fun and peaceful journey towards the places to visit in Bolu.

Places to Visit in Bolu

Karacasu Hot Springs

Karacasu Thermal Springs, which is very close to the city center, also cures many ailments such as skin and heart diseases, respiratory and digestive system diseases, bone and calcification.

Abant Lake

Abant Lake, which has a clear beauty in every season, can be a great route to spend a day or stay for a while and enjoy.
There are trees, fruits and herbs such as oak, beech, poplar, willow, hornbeam, ash, rosehip, fern, mint, strawberry, blackberry, ivy, raspberry, nettle around the lake.
Again, animals such as jackals, bears, foxes, wolves, roe deer, deer, squirrels and pigs live in the forests around the lake. It is possible to come across almost every bird species you can think of.


Yedigoller National Park

Yedigöller, which is frequently preferred especially by young people, is one of the most beautiful places that can be preferred for trekking, camping and picnic. But even if you don't have enough time to do these things, you should definitely spare 1-2 hours for a romantic walk.
You can view all Bolu Lakes in our Bolu Lakes article!(Turkish)


Kartalkaya Ski Center

If you want to visit this place and have a great time, there is no doubt that you will look forward to the opening of the winter season. Among the pine forests, on the white snow, you will feel the real excitement by integrating with nature.


Activities To Do in Bolu

You can go to Bolu whenever you find time, which offers the opportunity to do a different activity in every season of the year. In the summer season, you can camp in Abant, fish in Lake Abant and paraglide.
Also, do not forget to join the boat tours.

Skiing in Bolu in the winter season will add a different color to your life and you will be able to enjoy these natural beauties to the fullest. You can take a walk around the lakes in Bolu alone or with your loved ones, take part in bicycle tours and have a picnic in the permitted areas. In this city, which carries traces of history, you can learn different information about the city and take lots of photos, as there are many inns, baths and mosques. Goynuk should be your first stop if you intend to witness the sights that you cannot see everywhere and feed your eyes.

The historical houses in Göynük, besides being a hillside city, consist of the first settlements of the Turks in Anatolia, and when you see the view that it will offer you due to its location on the hillside, if you do not have a camera with you, you may be sad that you cannot immortalize this moment.

Food and Drink in Bolu

Although Bolu stands out more with its cooks rather than its historical local dishes, do not leave without tasting the taste of its dishes.
Let's take a look at these very special dishes with their history based on the palace cuisine, prepared by our world-famous Bolu chefs. First, go to Bolu Mengen Soup, and choose from their delicious soups; Mengen Soup, Cranberry Tarhana, Potato Soup.

Afterwards, for meals and desserts, you can choose from a variety of dishes and desserts from the skillful hands of the chefs of Mengen Aşçızade Restaurant, Manager Restaurant or Göynük Lalezar Restaurant: Halushka, Çığırtma, Mengen Rice, Bolu Beyi Dessert, Palace Halva.

Accommodation in Bolu

You can benefit from accommodation services with boutique hotels, chalets and old buildings. You just have to decide what you want.
If you want to relax in the forest and stay only with your loved ones, you can choose hotels and mansions in the form of mansions. But even if you say there is no need for excitement, Bolu Yıldız Hotel, Dorukkaya Ski & Mountain Resort and Büyük Abant Hotel, where you can stay in every quality you want, are among the places where visitors stay the most.
